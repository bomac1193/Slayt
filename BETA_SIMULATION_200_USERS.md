5. **Premium positioning.** $49/mo feels fair when you had to apply and wait. $49/mo feels expensive when you stumbled in from Google.

The beachhead (150K-500K creators earning $5K-20K/mo) can afford $49 but $149 makes them pause. The conviction-to-revenue loop needs VOLUME of posts to build the taste genome moat. Fewer users = slower data flywheel = weaker moat.

## BLIND SPOT ANALYSIS (From Simulated Feedback)

### Blind Spot 1: "Conviction Score Feels Arbitrary" (CRITICAL)
**Source:** 34% of Year 1 users
**Feedback:** "You gave me a 45 but didn't explain why. Am I supposed to trust a number?"
**Risk:** If users don't trust the score, they override it constantly, defeating the purpose.
**Fix:** **Conviction explainability.** Show component breakdown: "Taste: 72, Performance: 28, Brand: 61. Your performance score is low because the hook relies on a trend that peaked 3 weeks ago." This is the #1 feature request and the #1 blind spot.

### Blind Spot 2: "My Best Content Got Blocked" (HIGH)
**Source:** 18% of users experienced a false positive (high-performing post blocked by conviction gate)
**Feedback:** "Slayt blocked my video. I overrode it, posted anyway, and it got 500K views. Your AI is wrong."
**Risk:** Even ONE false positive that goes viral destroys trust. Creator tells everyone.
**Fix:** **Post-performance feedback loop.** When an overridden post performs well, automatically adjust the model. Show the user: "You were right â€” we've updated your conviction model based on this post's performance."

### Blind Spot 3: "Where Are My Old Posts?" (MEDIUM)
**Source:** 22% of users after 3+ months
**Feedback:** "I've posted 200 things through Slayt. Where's my archive? I can't find that carousel from October."
**Risk:** Media library is built but not prominent enough. Content gets lost.
**Fix:** Raise media library visibility. Add conviction-scored search: "Show me my posts that scored 80+ in the last 90 days."

### Blind Spot 4: "TikTok Conviction â‰  Instagram Conviction" (MEDIUM)
**Source:** 28% of cross-platform users
**Feedback:** "Same video scores 75 on Instagram and 52 on TikTok. Why? What do I change?"
**Risk:** Without platform-specific explanations, cross-platform conviction feels random.
**Fix:** Platform-specific conviction breakdown. "On TikTok, your opening 1 second matters more. This video's first frame is a title card â€” TikTok penalizes static openings."

### Blind Spot 5: "I Can't Get My Invite" (OPPORTUNITY)
**Source:** Waitlist applicants
**Feedback:** "I applied 2 months ago. Still waiting. I'm going to just use Later."
**Risk:** Waitlist too long = lost users to competitors.
**Fix:** Referral-based priority. Existing users can invite 3 people/month. This maintains exclusivity while accelerating growth through trusted networks.

### Blind Spot 6: "PNGs and Non-JPEG Formats" (LOW-MEDIUM)
**Source:** 8% of uploads
**Feedback:** "I uploaded a PNG and the colors shifted" or "WebP files don't upload"
**Risk:** The JPEG compression pipeline converts everything to JPEG. Some creators work in PNG for quality.
**Fix:** Detect format and preserve when possible. Only compress to JPEG when exceeding size limits.

### Blind Spot 7: "No Undo After Posting" (LOW)
**Source:** 5% of users
**Feedback:** "I posted through Slayt and realized I had a typo. Can't recall it."
**Risk:** Low frequency but high pain when it happens.
**Fix:** Not Slayt's problem â€” platform limitation. But could add: "Post review period: 60-second delay before actually publishing. Cancel within 60s."
